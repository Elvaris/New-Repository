Greek Salad

Ingredients
Tomato, cucumber, olives, feta cheese, onion, olive oil and oregano.
Preparation
Step 1: Chop the tomatoes, cucumber, and onions.
Step 2: Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
Step 3: Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle oregano.
Step 4: Gently and serve fresh.
